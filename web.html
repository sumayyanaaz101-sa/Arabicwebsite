Background & qualifications

With a Master's degree in Arabic and over 11 years of teaching experience, I specialise in guiding students from basic to advanced levels. I have a deep passion for making Arabic accessible to all learners, wherever they are in the world.

A notable achievement

I have translated two volumes of the renowned Arabic grammar series 'Lisan ul Quran' from Urdu to Arabic — a testament to my command of the language and dedication to Quranic education.
